What is Translation Consistency?

Translation consistency refers to the practice of ensuring that specific terms, phrases, and stylistic choices are rendered uniformly throughout a translated document. It ensures that the same source concept is always represented by the same target term, preventing confusion and maintaining a high professional standard.
